Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 10, 2026

Tee-Time.io (“we”, “us”) helps golfers search Utah tee times and receive optional alerts when matching times open. This policy describes what we collect and how we use it.

Information we collect

  • Account: email address and display name when you sign up (Google OAuth or email/password via Supabase).
  • Alert preferences: courses, dates, time windows, and player counts you save when creating tee time alerts.
  • Phone number: only if you choose SMS or email+SMS alerts. We verify ownership with a one-time code (Twilio Verify).
  • Usage: anonymous analytics (PostHog) to improve the product — not sold to third parties.

How we use SMS

Transactional alerts only. If you opt in on your Account page, we send text messages when tee times matching your saved alerts open or reopen. We do not send marketing or promotional SMS.

Frequency: varies with your alert settings and tee sheet activity (typically zero to a few messages per week per active alert).

Opt out: switch alert channel to Email only on Account, pause or remove alerts, or reply STOP to any message. Reply HELP for support.

Cost: message and data rates may apply per your carrier.

Email alerts

If you enable email alerts, we send transactional messages to your account email via Resend when matching tee times are found. You can disable alerts anytime on Account.

Sharing

We use service providers to operate the product: Supabase (database/auth), Cloudflare (hosting), Resend (email), Twilio (SMS verification and delivery), and Google (Places photos). We do not sell your personal information.

Data retention

Account and alert data are kept while your account is active. You may delete alerts on Account or contact us to request account deletion.

Contact

Questions: support@tee-time.io
